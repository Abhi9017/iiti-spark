# Operator and moderation checklist

## Before opening registration

- Obtain written institutional authorization and branding approval.
- Name the legal/operator entity and a responsible administrator.
- Publish operator contact, privacy contact, report response expectations, and emergency guidance.
- Confirm the service is restricted to adults aged 18+.
- Review applicable privacy, intermediary, campus conduct, harassment, and data-retention requirements with qualified counsel.
- Replace generic Terms/Privacy text with approved documents.
- Test deletion, blocking, report review, account suspension, and backup restoration.

## Access control

- Use a dedicated operator Google Workspace account.
- Restrict the database folder to the smallest possible moderator group.
- Review Drive access at least monthly.
- Never share raw database exports over personal email or chat.
- Do not let moderators use profile data for personal discovery or contact.

## Report workflow

The `REPORTS` sheet records status as `OPEN` by default. Define documented statuses such as:

- `OPEN`
- `TRIAGED`
- `ACTIONED`
- `NO_ACTION`
- `ESCALATED`
- `CLOSED`

Record only necessary moderator notes. Do not promise confidentiality that cannot be technically or legally maintained. Escalate credible threats or emergencies through approved institutional/public procedures rather than attempting informal investigation.

## Data minimization

The prototype intentionally avoids phone numbers, exact birth dates, precise location, government identifiers, passwords, and payment data. Do not add them to Google Sheets.

Set a written retention period for inactive sessions, passes/likes, messages, reports, audit events, and deleted accounts. Automated anonymization should be tested before relying on it.

## Branding

The included logo is original. Keep “unofficial prototype” visible unless IIT Indore formally authorizes the service. Do not imply that Google or IIT Indore endorses the app.
